About Me

Willy Anicette

BIO

Are you looking to get a modern website up and going for your company?

Or maybe your website is outdated, and you want to breathe some new life into it?

Stick around, Willy is going to tell you a bit about himself and how you can work together. With Willy on your team, he’ll get your website up with modern functionalities, optimized and responsive for mobile; along with a new coat of paint that’ll bring an increase in traffic with new business.

Over the years, Willy has spent his time learning from minds such as Mosh Hamedani from Udemy and Brad Traversy from YouTube. Willy has taken a plethora of tutorials from various sites including freeCodeCamp.org and LearnCode.academy. With the 2hr+ commute to and from work, he also supplements his learning from podcasts such as the Syntax Podcast with Wes Bos & Scott Tolinski and the .NET Rocks podcast with Carl Franklin & Richard Campbell. Willy is also involved with the Palm Beach Tech communities’ monthly meetups. It helps him stay consistent and continuously growing with like-minded people who love to code. This consistent education has taught Willy Front End and Back End languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, ASP.Net, and C#.

When building projects for businesses, it’s important to have multiple perspectives to better build the project. So, listening to Ted Talks and to various interviews from entrepreneurs and CEO’s helps to frame his mind into certain business needs he wouldn’t have thought of otherwise.

With the fast-paced evolution of technology, an additional way Willy believes he can stay up to speed is by actively pursuing certifications. His goals are to receive the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript Certifications from w3schools.com and acquire the Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate: Web Applications from Microsoft.

Willy has been influenced by technology from a very young age. This has cultivated a tremendous passion for technology which led him to go to college for Computer Networking. This transported him into the world of coding or “Sudoku”. Willy's love for music through playing the bass guitar has assisted him in bringing some creativity over into his logical coding world. Above all else, he also believes one way to keep his life balanced is by incorporating spirituality into his Sunday events. He keeps himself grounded by spending time with his family and friends.

Willy looks forward to working with you. He hopes to help bring solutions to the shortcomings you may be experiencing on your website and to see the increase in traffic you’ve been looking for!
